What Does AccuHit Do?
 The AccuHit allows for a constant mildly firm grip to be placed on the club, which in turn puts an individualized feel for each club.  The AccuHit becomes the resting point for the thumb and index finger for both left, and right handed golfers.  This repositioning of the thumb and index finger reduces the amount of unnecessary control these two fingers exert on the club.

SunGolf Magazine calls the AccuHit a "small price to pay for the confidence necessary to pull off that next shot for money." - Read On

Historically
 Traditionally many golfers overuse their thumb and forefinger, which in turn exerts and dominates their entire swing. This excessive tension triggers the major muscle groups of that side of the body and contributes to poor swing performance as well as a loss of club speed, clubface aperture and square-ness through the downswing.  Minimizing the amount of control of the index finger and forefinger with AccuHit will result in the desired ball flight, direction and distance.
