What is the AccuHit Grip?

It has taken five years to create, patent, trademark and package the AccuHit.  Many people have used the AccuHit and have found that it can definitely improve their score, especially regarding distance and accuracy.  The AccuHit allows the golfer to gain a feel for each club by providing a constant mildly firm grip.  This is brought about by keeping the shoulders, arms and hands relaxed throughout the swing. 

Where Are We Based?

The Southwest United States is a mecca for golf enthusiasts and has been a great market for this revolutionary golf training aid.  Thousands of golfers come to Arizona each year for its beautiful golfing weather.  This is one reason the AccuHit company is based in Arizona.
