July 11, 2001
Yes, we're still here.  We've had a few setbacks, being some upcoming clients, that will postpone our site release.  That may sound strange, but a client project is much more important than our own website.

Our "new look" is going to be one worth the wait, so please do not lose faith.
 
June 17, 2001
 Our server issue is finally resolved.  We are undergoing completion of a more applicable site design, in hopes of showing our potential to a more fluid audience.  Our failure to compromise has left us at a less than satisfactory financial end.  However, we WILL bounce back, and we will bounce back strong.
 
May 2001
 DynamixPro servers were going down at at an unacceptable rate.  To be perfectly honest, for them to go down at all is uncalled for.  Needless to say, we took care of the issue, and are now on a much more stable, reliable backend host.  The result of our uptime failure was poor response time, and delayed client project completion. 

We had to take care of inner company issues as well, and are working our way to a more financially sturdy solution, in hopes of benefiting from this financial quarter.
