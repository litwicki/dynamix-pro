Our optimum goal is to help our clients as much as possible, and in order to do that, we do our best to give our clients a general idea of what they're paying for, it only makes sense.  Below are a list of common questions to help you get an idea of what exactly we do, and how we take care of things.  Please feel free to contact help@dynamixpro.com if you have any questions that were not answered below.
Where can I find..?
Anything and everything related to DynamixPro can be found at our Site Map.
How much would it cost for you to design my website?  It's just for my own use, it's not a business site.
We don't charge our clients a flat fee, because that's unfair in our eyes.  It's impossible to label any service with a straight-forward price because time is always a factor.  If you were to fill out a price quote and let us know more information about your specific website, we could give you a much more accurate estimate.
Is there any way you can design my site for free?
Probably not.  We can't afford to develop an entire site for free, unless of course you can guarantee us some massive amounts of advertising.  Talk to jake@dynamixpro.com for more information on that.
My company needs business cards, and printable design, can you design that, or do you only focus on website design?
We can do anything you want us to do, except cook a turkey dinner.  If you need business cards made (or whatever the case may be), we will not only design them for you, but also have them printed and delivered to you as well.
